Information

Arc Achievers is a terraria-like roleplay in Mindustry. It has many features and game mechanics starting from the character system, the combat system, and many crafting utilities in your need.
The current guide at best, takes a while to read and is hard to memorize/access (since you would switch between the guide and the roleplay), which is why this guide has been made.
This guide was made for Arc Achievers v4.5
